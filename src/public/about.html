How It Works

Three tarot cards are drawn and placed from left to right. Each card represents a specific aspect of the topic. The positions (e.g., Past – Present – Future) can be customized to suit different questions.

Popular Interpretations

Card 1: Past; Present; You / The Other Person; Mind

Card 2: Present; Situation; Challenge; Body

Card 3: Future; Advice; Advice; Spirit

The Three-Card Spread is one of the most fundamental layouts in tarot reading, providing a structured glimpse into the past, present, and future. It is commonly used to develop interpretation skills and build a deeper understanding of how individual cards interact within a reading.
