How It Works

Three tarot cards are drawn and placed from left to right. Each card represents a specific aspect of the topic.

The positions (Past, Present, Future) help structure your interpretation.

Ready to explore the Three-Card Spread and uncover insights to guide your journey? Let's get started!

Popular Interpretations

  • Card ONE: Reflects the Past, highlighting important events and influences, or represents dynamics between You and Others.
  • Card TWO: Represents the Present situation, focusing on current challenges and the obstacles you may face.
  • Card THREE: Symbolizes the Future, offering advice and insights on potential outcomes and paths forward.
