Advanced Tarot Concepts

The Four Elements in Tarot

Wands (Fire)

Represents creativity, passion, and inspiration. Associated with the spiritual realm and new beginnings.

Cups (Water)

Symbolizes emotions, intuition, and relationships. Connected to the emotional realm and matters of the heart.

Swords (Air)

Represents thoughts, communication, and challenges. Associated with the mental realm and decision-making.

Pentacles (Earth)

Symbolizes material matters, work, and physical reality. Connected to the physical realm and practical concerns.

Numerology in Tarot

Aces (1)

New beginnings, potential, and opportunities. The purest form of each suit's energy.

Court Cards

Pages: New energy, learning
Knights: Action, movement
Queens: Nurturing, wisdom
Kings: Authority, mastery

10s

Completion, fulfillment, and the end of a cycle. Often represents the culmination of a journey.
